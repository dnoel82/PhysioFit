Our Story

About A&E PhysioFit@Home

A&E PhysioFit@Home was founded with a clear mission: to bring high-quality, personalized physical therapy directly into patients' homes across Greater Boston and the North Shore of Massachusetts.

We believe that exceptional care shouldn't require you to leave the comfort of your home. Our licensed therapists are specialists in home visitations — creating an intimate, one-on-one therapeutic environment that is fully tailored to your individual needs.

With over 20 years of combined medical field experience, our team knows how to navigate every aspect of your care — from treatment protocols to insurance and billing, we have you covered.
