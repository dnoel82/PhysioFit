Chronic Joint Pain

In-Home Chronic Joint Pain Treatment in North Shore & Boston

Chronic joint pain can arise from various conditions including arthritis, lupus, osteoporosis, or overuse injuries. At A&E PhysioFit@Home, a comprehensive evaluation by our therapists will identify the specific cause behind your discomfort — forming the foundation of a personalized treatment plan.

Our primary differentiator is our in-home treatment model — ensuring you receive expert care in the comfort of your home, eliminating the stress of traveling. While joint pain is more common in older individuals, it can affect people of all ages, and A&E PhysioFit@Home caters to patients of all age groups.
