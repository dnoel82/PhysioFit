Neck Pain Treatment

In-Home Neck Pain Treatment in North Shore & Boston

Neck pain can arise from various factors — poor posture, muscle strains, worn joints, nerve compression, injuries, and certain diseases. Our expert therapists begin with a comprehensive evaluation, discussing your symptoms, medical history, and lifestyle to identify the origin and severity of your pain.

Treatment may include a combination of manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, posture education, ergonomic advice, and pain-relieving modalities — all tailored to your specific needs. We also provide specialized treatment for neck pain resulting from injuries, accidents, or whiplash.
