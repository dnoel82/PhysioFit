Knee Pain Treatment

In-Home Knee Pain Treatment in North Shore MA

Chronic knee pain can result from arthritis, tendonitis, bursitis, ligament tears, or underlying diseases. Our comprehensive in-home assessment will help determine the specific cause of your pain — forming the basis of a personalized treatment plan designed to restore function and reduce pain.

Our in-home treatments allow patients to receive therapy in their familiar environment, which often enhances relaxation and healing. We also offer post-surgical rehabilitation, ensuring you regain strength, mobility, and function following any knee-related surgery.
