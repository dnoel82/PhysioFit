Service Details

Our Core Service Programs

Wellness Services

Wellness is an optimum level of health and fitness that allows you to function fully across all areas of life — physical, emotional, spiritual, social, intellectual and environmental. Maintaining wellness includes proper nutrition, sleeping habits, regular exercise, and avoiding substance use — without pain.

Total Body Diagnostic Service

If you've been worried about a nagging pain or unsure if you need treatment for a tight area, our total body diagnostic is for you. One of our PTs will assess your total body mobility, strength, and spinal segmental mobility to determine the best course of action.

Medical Concierge Services

We have been in the medical field for over 20 years. Whether your questions are insurance and billing related, medical language interpretations, or you need hands-on bedside support — we will help coordinate services, assess your personal in-home needs, support your emotional concerns, and alleviate caregiver stress.

Sports & Orthopedic Rehab

Post-surgical recovery, musculoskeletal conditions, and sports injuries treated in the comfort of your home. Our therapists are equipped to handle a wide range of orthopedic conditions — from simple strains to complex post-operative rehabilitation.
