Back Pain Treatment

Expert In-Home Back Pain Relief in North Shore & Boston

Back pain can arise from various sources — muscle strains, herniated discs, spinal stenosis, poor posture, or underlying conditions like arthritis. At A&E PhysioFit@Home, our approach prioritizes in-home care, bringing therapy to your doorstep with individualized treatment plans crafted by expert therapists.

We employ a blend of manual techniques, therapeutic exercises, and educational strategies to ensure comprehensive care. Early intervention can be key to preventing minor issues from escalating — and therapeutic exercises and postural training can fortify your back against future pain.
